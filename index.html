About Me
"Every wedding tells its own story — I simply give it the rhythm, light, and emotion it deserves."

I'm Anik Manna, a wedding video editor devoted to turning raw footage into cinematic memories. My work is guided by one belief — a wedding film should feel less like a recording and more like a story, carrying the same warmth, laughter, and quiet emotion that filled the day itself.

Every project I take on is treated with care, from the first cut to the final grade. I focus on pacing, color, and sound in equal measure, blending them until the film feels seamless and alive. My goal is always the same: to hand couples a film they'll return to again and again, one that grows more precious with every year that passes.
